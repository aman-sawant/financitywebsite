About Us

Financity is the official finance club of Bharati Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be) University, College of Engineering, Pune. We aim to promote financial literacy among students through interactive workshops, engaging events, and insightful resources.

Our activities focus on simplifying concepts like personal finance, investments, and financial planning. We strive to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, empowering students to make informed financial decisions.

Join us at Financity to explore, learn, and grow in the exciting world of finance!
